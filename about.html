About

It can be difficult to find music based on how you're feeling, and even more so if you're unsure what you're feeling. By using Mood Music, you let us handle that for you. Using an emotion detection API provided through Kairos, we give you music to match. It's pretty awesome honestly.

Mood Music was designed and created by Team Mosaic in July 2018. Tara Bender, Andrew Carr, and Giacomo Fernandez Giganti.
